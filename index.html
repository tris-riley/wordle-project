Find The Word

Rules


Simlar to the classic game Wordle, the aim here is to find a random 5-letter word using 6 guesses or less.
Use the on screen keyboard to input your letter selections.

1. Correctly placed letters will turn the tile green.

2.Letters that are in the word but in the wrong position will turn yellow.

3.Incorrect letters will turn grey.

Be careful, the game will accept any combination of letters whether they form a valid word or not.
When the game is over, refresh the page to have another go. Good luck!
